# HubSpot Playbooks

Practical guides for setting up and running HubSpot. From zero to a production CRM with AI automation, without paying for expensive tiers.

## Tracks

- [CRM Setup](./crm-setup/) - Configuring HubSpot from scratch the right way
- [Custom Properties](./custom-properties/) - Property schemas for AI and email tracking
- [n8n Integrations](./n8n-integrations/) - Connecting HubSpot to n8n and external tools

## Why HubSpot + n8n

HubSpot's native workflows are limited on free and Starter tiers. n8n connects to HubSpot's API directly and unlocks AI-powered logic, cross-platform triggers, and custom automation that HubSpot can't do natively.

The combination gives you enterprise-level automation at a fraction of the cost. Most of the patterns in this folder work on HubSpot's free tier.
